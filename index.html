Basic Care Tips

💧 Water Quality

Regular water changes (10-25% weekly) and a good filtration system are essential for a healthy tank.

🌡️ Temperature

Keep tropical fish between 75-80°F (24-27°C). Always use a reliable aquarium heater and thermometer.

🐟 Feeding

Feed your fish high-quality flakes or pellets 1-2 times a day. Only feed what they can eat in 2 minutes.
